Members joining details

Members who join TEAM have to commit to the below objective and work towards the success metrics.

Types of Members Possible
Startups
Full Time Members
Nominee Members
Majority of the Employees startup are in Mumbai
Membership criteria for Startup members is to satisfy at least 2 of the 3 criteria laid out below
Headquarters of the Startup is in Mumbai
Majority of the Employees of the Startup are in Mumbai
CEO is based in Mumbai
After clearance of the above criteria, startups will apply for members based on below to Join as Full Time Members
Operational for a minimum of 3 years
  • Valued at more than $100M OR recorded more than $20M in Revenue as of previous financial year
  • Contribute 5L INR every year to TEAM Corpus
  • Total Members who can be Full Time is fixed at a maximum of 45 as of CY23. The limit to Full time Members will be reviewed in Dec of every year and the Governing Council (defined below) can vote on increasing the same. In case the number of nominations in a particular year exceeds the maximum number of allowed full time members the outgoing Governing Council will elect the maximum number allowed from the applicants.
  • Members can nominate themselves to different committees / work streams through the year. Memberships will not be renewed automatically. Membership will be for a period of 12 months (January to December) and continuation will be evaluated by the end of every year (December) based on their participation and/or contribution to TEAM initiatives. Full time membership to TEAM is limited to a maximum of 5 consecutive years.
Governing Council
Upon finalising the Full Time Members of TEAM for a CY, the Governing Council of 9-12 members will be elected. The same will be headed by a President and a Vice President. The GC term will be for a period of 12 months (January to December) and a new GC will be nominated /elected every year in Jan after the Full time Members have been finalised every year. Elected members to the Governing Council cannot serve for more than 3 consecutive years. The Governing council slots will have a maximum of 8 full time members and a minimum of 1 nominee member and 1 ecosystem enabler.
Operational for a minimum of 2 years
  • Valued at more than $10M OR recorded more than $2M in Revenue as of previous financial year
  • Contribute 50,000 INR every year to TEAM Corpus
  • Total Members who can be Nominee Members to be fixed at maximum of 5 as of CY23
  • The limit to Nominee Members will be reviewed in Dec of every year and the Governing Council can vote on increasing the same
  • A nominee member by definition will have to be nominated by a Full time member. The reason to have Nominee members is to get some young enthusiastic founders to start contributing to the TEAM efforts.
  • The period for nomination will be open from 1st Jan to 31st Jan every year where Full time members can nominate members for this pool. The Governing council of TEAM will select from the pool and confirm the final membership for the year for these Nominated Members.
  • Nominated members by definition need to put in work across streams through the year. These memberships will not be renewed automatically. Membership will be for a period of 12 months (January to December) and continuation will be evaluated by the end of every year (December) based on the contribution to the objective to TEAM. This evaluation will be done by the Governing Council of TEAM.
Criteria for Ecosystem Enablers to Join
  • Contribute 10L INR every year to TEAM Corpus
  • Calendarized yearly plan presented to TEAM on helping Mumbai startups through events before joining / renewal every year and approved by the Governing Council
  • The plan has to included hosting a minimum of 1 event every year
  • Be part of the sponsorship for the Annual Flagship Event of team
  • Total Members who can be Ecosystem Enablers to be fixed at a maximum of 10 as of CY23
  • The limit for Ecosystem Enablers will be reviewed in Dec of every year and the Governing Council can vote on increasing the same
