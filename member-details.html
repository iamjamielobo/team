Let's scale Mumbai together.

Annual membership is now available, enabling us to work together towards our shared objectives and defined work practices.

Startup Members

The membership criteria for Startup members is to achieve at least 2 of the 3 from below:

  • Headquarters of the Startup should be in Mumbai
  • Majority of the employees of the Startup should be present in Mumbai
  • The CEO should be based in Mumbai
Types of Startup membership
Full Time Members
  • The company should be operational for a minimum of 3 years
  • It should bevalued at more than $100M or recorded more than $20M in revenue as of previous financial year
  • It should contribute INR 5L every year to TEAM Corpus
Nominee Members
  • The company should be operational for a minimum of 2 years
  • It should be valued at more than $10M OR recorded more than $2M in revenue as of previous financial year
  • It should contribute INR 50,000 every year to TEAM Corpus
  • The total members who can be Nominee Members to be fixed at maximum of 5 as of CY23
  • The limit to Nominee Members will be reviewed in December of every year and the Governing Council can vote on increasing the same

Total members who can be Full Time is fixed at a maximum of 45 as of CY23. The limit of Full Time members will be reviewed in December of every year and the Governing Council (defined below) can vote on increasing the same. In case the number of nominations in a particular year exceeds, the maximum number of allowed full time members the outgoing Governing Council will elect the maximum number allowed from the applicants.

Members can nominate themselves to different committees / work streams through the year. Memberships will not be renewed automatically. Membership will be valid for a period of 12 months (January to December) and continuation will be evaluated by the end of every year (December) based on their participation and/or contribution to TEAM initiatives. Full Time membership to TEAM is limited to a maximum of 5 consecutive years.

Startup Ecosystem Enablers
  • Contribute 10L INR every year to TEAM Corpus
  • Calendarized yearly plan presented to TEAM on helping Mumbai startups through events before joining / renewal every year and approved by the Governing Council
  • The plan has to included hosting a minimum of 1 event every year
  • Be part of the sponsorship for the Annual Flagship Event of team
  • Total Members who can be Ecosystem Enablers to be fixed at a maximum of 10 as of CY23
  • The limit for Ecosystem Enablers will be reviewed in Dec of every year and the Governing Council can vote on increasing the same
Governing Council
Upon finalising the Full Time Members of TEAM for a CY, the Governing Council of 9-12 members will be elected. The same will be headed by a President and a Vice President. The Governing Council's term will be for a period of 12 months (January to December) and a new Governing Council will be nominated /elected every year in January after the Full Time members have been finalised every year. Elected members to the Governing Council cannot serve for more than three consecutive years. The Governing council slots will have a maximum of 8 full time members and a minimum of 1 nominee member and 1 ecosystem enabler. Please check the latest Governing Council details here.
